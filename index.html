about

I'm a filmmaker based in London, UK. I specialise in research film projects and short documentary films, exploring human experience and connecting viewers to powerful stories. I aim to get to the heart of an issue or story by spending time and care in the field, telling personal stories as a collaborative process. I provide professional story-driven content production, videography and editing services. For all inquiries, please get in contact below.
